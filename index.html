Django Girls

If you are a female and you want to learn how to make websites, we have good news for you!

We are helding one-day workshops for beginners!

It will take place on 21st of July in Berlin, just before a big IT conference: EuroPython which gathers a lot of talented programmers from all over the world.

We believe that IT industry will greatly benefit from bringing more women into technology. We want to give you an opportunity to learn how to program and become one of us - female programmers!

Workshops are free of charge and if you can’t afford coming to Berlin, but you are very motivated to learn and then share your knowledge with others, we have some funds to help you out with your travel costs and accommodation. Don’t wait too long - you can apply for a pass only until 30th of June!

Apply for a pass!

If you are a woman, you know English and have a laptop - you can apply for a pass! You don’t need to know any technical stuff - workshops are for people who are new to programming.

As a workshop attendee you will:

  • attend one-day Django workshops during which you will create your first website
  • get a free EuroPython ticket (which normally costs 400 €!), where you can meet people from the industry and learn more about programming
  • be fed by us - during workshops and EuroPython food is provided

We have space only for 20 people, so make sure to fill the form very carefully!

If you cannot afford a trip to Berlin, but you really want to attend - we can help. Thanks to EuroPython and Django Software Foundation, we have some funds for financial aid. Please let us know if you need financial support to get to Berlin. In return we will expect that after workshops you will share a knowledge you gained with your local community. You can give a talk during a university class, organise a small meeting where you show people stuff you’ve learn or anything that will convince people to get involved in programming.

Application are now open!

You can now apply for a place during workshops as well as financial grants to come to EuroPython. Application process closes on June 21st and you'll be informed about acceptance or rejection by June 30th.
