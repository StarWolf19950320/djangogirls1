Order Django Girls Workshop Box for your event!

Get a golden envelope full of Django Girls stickers, gold letter balloons, stickers of our partners, buttons and nametags.
Everything you need to make your workshop DjangoGirlsy!

How it works?

We purchased Django Girls goodies in bulk quantity, which massively lowered their price. This means we can better use everyone's money and time spent on those kind of things. Now we're gonna ship it from London to Django Girls workshops from all over the world to so you can all spend your sponsors money better! YAY!

We calculated that one event would need to pay around $150 USD for all of those things, and sometimes they're hard to get. We're able to buy and ship them all for around $50.

What's in the box?

The shiny beautiful envelope contains many awesome things:

  • Django Girls logo stickers
  • YAY! stickers
  • Pixel Heart stickers
  • Some stickers from our global partners
  • Sticky orange nametags
  • Gold letters DJANGOGIRLS balloons, 16 inches tall
  • Orange Django Girls logo buttons
  • Little surprise, sometimes, maybe? ;-)

How much is it?

Free! But. For our first trial period, we ordered enough goodies for about 3000 people attending our events. This purchase has been covered by donations made to Django Girls Foundation, and it means we want to keep the boxes free for everyone. However going forward we need to make it sustainable. That's why we ask for an optional donation. If your event can afford to cover the costs of the box (~$50), we kindly ask you to donate. If you have money leftover from the workshop: donate it and pay it forward to send boxes to those events that cannot yet afford it.

I want it!

Yay, that's great! In order to receive it, there are few requirements you need to meet:

  • Application process for your event needs to already be open
  • You need to order it at least 6 weeks before workshop to allow for shipping time

One little thing: during the trial period, we can only send one free box per city. If you want another one, we will ask you for a donation of $50. Thanks for understanding!
