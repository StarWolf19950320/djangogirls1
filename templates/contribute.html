Contribute

There are many ways to contribute to Django Girls: you can organize an event, coach, work on the tutorial and help on Gitter. If you don't have time but want to help, you can always support us on Patreon.

Organize!

Each Django Girls event is a free, one-day workshop for 30-60 people on HTML, CSS, Python and Django. A typical workshop takes 8 hours and people work in small, 3-person groups with a coach dedicated to each group.

Want to get started? Check our Organizer's Manual and bring Django Girls to your city!

Coach!

You have just heard that an event is happening in your city and went to help? Contact the local organizing team and ask if they need more coaches!

You will help a 3-person group to install what they need for the event and guide them through our tutorial.

Don't forget to check our Coach Manual!

Improve the tutorial!

Something in the tutorial is not clear or there is a typo? You can improve the tutorial by making pull request on our Github repository.

You can also work on the translation of the tutorial. If you want to join a team or launch a new translation, contact us.

Work on the website!

There is always something to do to make our website better. You can report bugs and typo, fix those or work on new features.

We are waiting for your pull requests!

Help on Gitter!

Many people are doing our tutorial outside of a workshop. Sometimes, they get stuck and need help. How about spending a few hours in a week to help them?

This Gitter room is designed to be a friendly space, especially to beginner. Remember that our Code of Conduct also apply there.

Want to do more?

You want to do something and it's not list here? You can look at our Trello board and propose new projects to work on.
